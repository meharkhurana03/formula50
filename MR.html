The team generated huge excitement from the off with the sensational return of Michael Schumacher, but headlines soon followed on track: three podiums in their debut season, all via Nico Rosberg - who then claimed a breakthrough pole/victory double at China in 2012. And with Hamilton now partnered by steely Finn Valtteri Bottas, Mercedes remain very much the team to beat.
